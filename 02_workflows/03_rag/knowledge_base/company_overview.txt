NovaTech Solutions is a fictional cloud-based project management company founded in 2023.

Company Overview:
NovaTech Solutions provides AI-powered project management tools for remote teams. Our flagship product is "NovaBoard," a smart Kanban board that uses machine learning to predict task completion times and automatically reassign overdue tasks.

Pricing Plans:
- Starter Plan: $9/month per user. Includes up to 5 projects, basic Kanban board, and email support.
- Pro Plan: $29/month per user. Includes unlimited projects, AI task predictions, sprint planning, and priority chat support.
- Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing. Includes everything in Pro, plus SSO, dedicated account manager, custom integrations, and SLA guarantees.

Refund Policy:
NovaTech offers a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you are not satisfied, contact support@novatech.example.com within 30 days of your purchase for a full refund. Annual subscriptions can be refunded pro-rata within the first 60 days.

Data Security:
All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3. NovaTech is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Customer data is stored in AWS data centers in the US-East and EU-West regions. We perform daily backups with a 90-day retention policy.
