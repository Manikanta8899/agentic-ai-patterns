NovaTech Solutions - Technical Troubleshooting Guide

Issue: NovaBoard is running slowly
Solution: Clear your browser cache and cookies. Ensure you're using a supported browser (Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 15+, Edge 90+). If the issue persists, try disabling browser extensions. For teams with more than 50 members, consider enabling "Performance Mode" under Settings > Advanced > Performance. This limits background syncing to every 30 seconds instead of real-time.

Issue: Tasks are not syncing between devices
Solution: Ensure you have a stable internet connection. Check if you're logged into the same account on all devices. Force a manual sync by going to Settings > Sync > Force Sync. If using the mobile app in offline mode, ensure "Background App Refresh" is enabled on your device. Sync conflicts are resolved using a "last write wins" strategy.

Issue: AI predictions seem inaccurate
Solution: The AI model requires at least 4 weeks of historical data and a minimum of 50 completed tasks to make reliable predictions. Ensure tasks are being marked as "Done" promptly. If predictions are still off, go to Settings > AI > Recalibrate Model. This retrains the model using the last 12 weeks of data and takes approximately 2 hours.

Issue: Cannot connect Slack integration
Solution: You need admin permissions on your Slack workspace. Go to Settings > Integrations > Slack > Reconnect. Ensure your Slack workspace has not exceeded its integration limit (free Slack plans allow up to 10 integrations). If using Slack Enterprise Grid, you may need to whitelist NovaBoard in your Slack admin console under Approved Apps.

Issue: Export is not completing
Solution: Large exports (over 10,000 tasks) can take up to 1 hour. You will receive an email notification with a download link when the export is ready. Exports expire after 7 days. If the export still fails, try exporting one project at a time. Contact support if the issue persists.
